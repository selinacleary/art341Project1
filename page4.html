ABOUT THE WESTERN REDCEDAR

The Western Redcedar, or, Thuja pilicatais among the most prevalent of coniferous trees in the Northwestern parts of North America. It grows at altitudes up to 2,290 feet above sea level! They grow up to 230 feet tall. They can live as long as 1500 years-old.
