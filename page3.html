ABOUT THE GIANT SEQUOIA

The Giant Sequoia, or Sequoiadendron giganteum, is the largest living thing by volume, and can reach heights of 280 feet. The oldest living Giant Sequoia is 3,500 years-old. Because they are secluded to a very small area in California, they have adapted to forest fires!
